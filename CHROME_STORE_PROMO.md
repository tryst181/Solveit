# Solveit — Autonomous Form & Quiz Engine

Are you exhausted by repetitive quizzes, endless structural onboarding forms, and academic portals? **Solveit** is the world’s foremost agentic zero-footprint browser extension designed to instantly read, comprehend, and natively fill out complex DOM elements at superhuman speeds.

Backed by **any** LLM API key you provide (OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, or hundreds of free-tier OpenRouter models), Solveit grants absolute automation precision without ever compromising your local data or your API budget.

### ✨ Why Solveit?

- **Ghost-Level Stealth**: Unlike other extensions that leave messy HTML elements or overlay clunky interfaces, Solveit operates entirely in a closed programmatic envelope. It injects exactly zero custom `<style>` scopes, DOM wrappers, or tracking classes. It navigates native input fields as authentically and invisibly as possible.
- **Vision-Language Synthesis**: Running into circuit diagrams? Organic chemistry structures? Solveit reads and digests up to 5 on-page diagrams automatically, piping them straight to standard multi-modal engines like GPT-4o to derive the absolute mathematically correct answer before finding the correct `click` target. 
- **Dictate to the Memory Reservoir**: Why type forms? Tap the **Dictate with AI 🎤** button, casually tell the extension your Employee ID, College Major, or Emergency Contact details via voice, and watch as Solveit intelligently parses your transcript and permanently memorizes the exact, structured JSON needed to autocomplete academic and personal inputs moving forward.
- **Micro-Token Architecture**: Stop blowing limits and daily API caps! This engine relies on an advanced *Waterfall Cascade pattern*, isolating tokens to a single high-performance request path instead of blindly duplicating prompts. If your primary AI rate-limits, it silently and automatically defaults to your backup provider instantly.

### ⚙️ How to use:
1. Hit **Dashboard** and paste your API keys securely in the beautiful, streamer-friendly glassmorphism UI.
2. Visit any Google Form, university quiz, or dynamic React portal.
3. Tap **Solve** from your extension menu (or hit `Alt+S`) and watch the magic happen off-screen.
4. Let the magic happen!
